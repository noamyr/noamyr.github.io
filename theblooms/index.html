The Blooms is a diagrammatic science fiction that uses multiple blooms—drastic shifts in populations of Azolla, tulips, and reindeer lichens, as well as speculative blooms—as its central narrative framework. Through an interactive web of entanglements, it maps the deep-time metabolic shifts, financial speculation, and ecological cycles that shape these blooms. The project critiques capitalist enclosures of nature, linking colonial trade, geoengineering, nitrogen crises, and speculative markets.

How to Use

This interface offers two navigation modes. In Guided Mode, the diagram progresses automatically along a suggested path. In Explore Mode, you can choose your own route by clicking on nodes that are highlighted in orange.

Throughout the journey, you’ll be able to identify your position by following the directional arrows: the currently narrated connection appears in red, upcoming possibilities are marked in orange, visited paths fade into purple, and untouched links remain gray.
